Add a Business
If you wish to add a business to this directory, please send an email to edougal@tfs.tamu.edu and provide information for as many of the following items as possible:

Name of company*
Full physical address, including zip code of manufacturing facility*
Texas county in which the manufacturing facility is located*
Contact name*
Contact telephone number*
Contact email
Web address
Type of wood products manufactured*
Wood species used*
Latitude/longitude coordinates

* Required fields
Following receipt of this email, contact will be made using the telephone number provided to verify the request, clarify information as needed, and, if necessary, discuss suitability of the request for this directory. If you wish to call first or to submit information by telephone you may do so using the telephone number provided on the page.
Update a Business
If you would like to update the information on your business, or believe the information for a business listed in the directory is incomplete or innacurate, please send an email to edougal@tfs.tamu.edu , and provide the updated information including a contact name and telephone number. Following receipt of this email, contact will be made using the telephone number provided to verify the request prior to revisions being made. If you wish to call first or to submit information by telephone you may do so using the telephone number provided on the page.
